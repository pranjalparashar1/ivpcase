Definitive Recommendation

Adopt the 'Blended Carry' Structure.

This model is unequivocally superior, offering the most robust framework for aligning interests, ensuring fairness, and guaranteeing the market viability of the Continuation Vehicle.

Maximum Alignment

A 100% GP rollover provides the strongest possible "skin in the game," directly linking the GP's financial success to that of its partners.

Unquestionable Fairness

The "status quo" option for rolling LPs honors their long-term commitment, a practical necessity to secure the 70% participation required.

Market Viability

A highly-aligned GP is the most sought-after feature for new secondary investors, making this the most credible and attractive structure for a successful capital raise.

Implementation Pathway.

A phased, partnership-driven approach to de-risk execution and accelerate time-to-market for a firm-wide liquidity platform.

01

PHASE 1 (1-2 MONTHS)

Strategy & Partner Selection

  • Engage specialized legal counsel to design the token's legal framework (e.g., Reg D compliance).
  • Conduct due diligence and select a full-service, regulated tokenization platform partner.
  • Finalize the strategic and economic structure of the tokenized feeder fund.

02

PHASE 2 (2-3 MONTHS)

Technology & Issuance

  • Collaborate with partner to deploy and rigorously audit smart contracts on a public L2 blockchain (e.g., Polygon).
  • Integrate the platform's investor portal with FinValley's KYC/AML compliance workflows.
  • Mint the digital tokens representing ownership in the feeder fund.

03

PHASE 3 (1-2 MONTHS)

Offering & Onboarding

  • Offer existing LPs the option to sell their position or roll into the tokenized format.
  • Market the offering to new, qualified accredited investors via the platform's network.
  • Manage investor subscription agreements, funding, and the whitelisting of digital wallets.

04

PHASE 4 (ONGOING)

Secondary Trading & Management

  • List the token for secondary trading on a regulated Alternative Trading System (ATS).
  • Utilize the platform for digital fund administration, including cap table, distributions, and reporting.
  • Monitor market activity and ensure ongoing compliance with all regulatory requirements.

Navigating the Risks.

Our recommended approach is designed to mitigate key challenges, but vigilant oversight is critical for success.

Regulatory & Compliance Risk

Mitigated by engaging top-tier legal counsel and partnering with a regulated platform with a proven track record of compliant offerings.

Cybersecurity & Custody Risk

Mitigated via comprehensive smart contract audits and partnering with an enterprise-grade digital asset custodian to secure assets.

Market Adoption & Liquidity Risk

There is no guarantee a liquid market will form. This is mitigated by offering a high-quality asset and potentially engaging market makers.

Price Volatility Risk

Increased liquidity brings price volatility. This requires a robust investor education strategy to manage LP expectations.
